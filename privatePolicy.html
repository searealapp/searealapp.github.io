Privacy Policy

 

Overview


This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the SeaReal iPhone app.

 

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Application


We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in SeaReal.


Individual settings relating to the SeaReal app are not personal and are stored only on your device. You might also be asked to provide access to your photo library, but this is only so that you can open your photos and videos in SeaReal and save any color corrected media back to your library. We don’t process that information at all and have no access to it.

 

App Analytics


To help us better understand how people use the SeaReal app and to continue improving them, we use analytics information provided by App Store Connect. This service provides us with some generic usage, transaction, and other statistics that are completely anonymous and cannot personally identify you in any way.

 

Contact Us


If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to get in touch with us at searealapp@gmail.com.
