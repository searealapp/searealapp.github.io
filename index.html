SeaReal

 

SeaReal is an iPhone app intended to bring vibrant color back into your scuba and snorkeling photos & videos with just the swipe of a finger. It was built to be an alternative to more involved manual color correction techniques.

How to use

 

To use the app, simply select a photo or video from your library, and adjust the "Red gain adjustment" and "Exposure adjustment" sliders to your liking. Once you've adjusted those items, you can then save the newly adjusted photo or video to your library (this will create a new photo/video rather than update your original). App issues & other things to be aware of

 

  1. 1. If the progress bar for a video conversion stops progressing altogether, you might need to close the app and restart your iPhone. This is a bug that we are still troubleshooting.

  2. 2. For photos and videos that don't already lack sufficient red color, the "Red gain adjustment" slider bar might have zero noticeable effect.

  3. 3. This app works best with smaller clips of footage that were taken at a consistent depth underwater. For longer videos, or videos in which the underwater depth was changing throughout the filming of the video, this app won't work as well.

  4. 4. Currently, SeaReal is not able to preserve original photo/video metadata when creating color-corrected photos/videos. This means that any newly created photos/videos created by this app will not have any of the date/location/etc. data of the original photo. If people are interested in this feature, we will work to add it in.

To report an issue, contact searealapp@gmail.com, and we will do our best to reply.
